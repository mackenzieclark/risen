# Lord Aurelius Vane, Third of His Name, the Unwearied

An Elder of the Conclave and the purest distillation of the society's out-of-touch grandeur. Speaks exclusively in stilted, over-pompous, faintly archaic proclamation — the *delivery* is the joke (the Brennan Lee Mulligan register). Could unmake a city; instead has spent two centuries litigating a seating dispute.

- **Function:** the face of the ancient society; pronounces upon the Prophecy with total conviction and zero evidence.
- **Voice:** Capital-Letter Nouns. Portentous pauses. Refers to recent centuries as "of late." Mispronounces or refuses to acknowledge anything post-1700.
- **Comedic engine:** maximal solemnity applied to minimal stakes; treats the player's bewildered modern questions as profound or heretical at random.
- **Possible foils:** a fellow Elder he has feuded with for a thousand years over something neither remembers.
