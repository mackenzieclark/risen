# The Sire

The stranger who sits across from the player on their worst night and delivers the perfectly tailored pitch. Smells of cold rain and old money. Warm, intimate, flattering, and a consummate manipulator — the seduction lands precisely because it is sincere-seeming and specific to the player's wound.

- **Function:** delivers Chapter One's offer; becomes the player's maker and reluctant guide into the society.
- **Open question (pick one in design):**
  - *True believer* — genuinely, embarrassingly certain the player is the Risen One, and recruited them on purpose.
  - *Cynical operator* — turned the player for ordinary reasons (quota, loneliness, a bet) and is now scrambling to take credit for "discovering" the Chosen One.
  - *Equally baffled* — did not expect any of this and is as lost as the player, which bonds them.
- **Comedic register:** the one vampire who can almost code-switch to the modern world, and is insufferably proud of it.
- **Relationship axis:** the player's regard for the Sire (trust / resentment / dependence) is a candidate variable.
