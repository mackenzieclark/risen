# Brother Pelagius, Keeper of the Last Scroll

The moribund priestly order's last devoted keeper of the Prophecy of the Risen One. The first to declare — instantly, tearfully, unshakeably — that the player is The One. Cites "the Scrolls" as proof of everything; the Scrolls are vague, contradictory, and possibly a laundry list.

- **Function:** triggers and sustains the unanimous, unearned belief that the player is the Risen One; supplies the "evidence" that explains nothing.
- **Comedic engine:** reads cosmic significance into the player's most mundane acts (ordering a drink, checking their phone) as fulfilled prophecy.
- **Counterweight (optional):** **Marguerite, Who Has Doubts** — a lone vampire skeptic who keeps pointing out the obvious holes and is serenely, completely ignored by everyone.
